I am a research assistant and PhD student at the Visual Computing Institute at RWTH Aachen University, Germany, where I work with Professor Leif Kobbelt.

My research interests are in computer graphics and geometry processing, with a focus on surface parametrization. I enjoy applying numerical optimization techniques to geometric problems that have applications in 3D modeling or engineering. Currently, I am working on algorithms that generate high-quality maps between different surfaces. Before that, I designed new methods for semi-automatic quad meshing (retopology).
